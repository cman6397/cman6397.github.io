Chess

In 2014 I began playing online chess on chess.com after playing a couple games with friends in the dorm rooms. I wasn't very good but I really enjoyed playing and wanted to improve. I advanced from a 600 rating to a 2200 rating which was an improvement from the 5th percentile of players on the site to the 99.8th percentile. I now get paired with national masters and fide masters regularly and can hold my own against many of them in games of blitz!
